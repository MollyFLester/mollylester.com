HUBBLE

Hubble is a conceptual online learning platform that teaches coding through the creation of an intellectual community. As part of the exploratory project, my team was tasked with creating a better online learning experience using the design thinking approach. The website development process took us through research, synthesizing, wireframing, testing and finally the creation of UI.

RESEARCH

Using the agile methodology, my team began with a research plan to define a our lean UX strategy that would help us empathize with online learning users. In order to better understand the context of online learning we collected domain research through surveys of online learning users, completing a robust competitive analysis of current online sites, and then funneled that information into a SWOT analysis to determine places for innovation. In order to also gain the deeper qualitative data we conducted interviews with past, current, and potential users.

SYNTHESIS

Leveraging the data that was found throughout the research process, the team began to create “buckets” of information. By grouping the information, we saw that key patterns were being formed and we turned these into actionable design principles, personas, scenarios, and use cases.
